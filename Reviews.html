Boult Audio "Energizing" Truly wireless buds review (in-depth)

As you might have sensed from the title the word "energizing" well that is something reserved for the later part. But this my review of the Boult Audio TWS the midrange earphones by entry-level company name Boult. To start of first the Build quality of the earbuds is done quite well ,i have to say.One bud hardly weighs less than 3.0 grams in my rough estimate but they are quite light atleast in my ear it fits comfortably. though this might be different based on your ear structure and it might simply not be fatigue for quite a few you so they did a decent job on the build of the eaarbuds. Now coming to looks, here i like the fact it looks decent considering its capabilities but still the case actully looks too big and does not fit well in my pocket and jutts out so don't get me wrong it is not like it is bad considering what it does but astheicaly what i would call is it looks "ugly". Now talking about battery life here i would say they definately nailed as per my research it has 560 milli-amp-hour battery and i have to say it can last you as long as 2 days on a single charge and if you are a heavy user easily expect it to last for 1 and half day. coming to charging this thing supports a maximum charging of 2.5 watts per minute and i would like to say it can take more than 105 minutes as i timed it exactly,now i do like the fact it supports bridge charging or 2-way charging that means it can charge itself fully with the provided usb type-c port but you do also get a provision of a usb- port which can help your current device give a kick start in an emergency instance or possibility therefore tiny peice of tech acts as a powerbank too, hence title of the review is "Energizing" this one of the stand out feature that actually blew my mind. Finally talking about Sound quality and i will divide between 3 catagories Bass= Here i would definately say the bass is a lot more exagerrated as they claim on their website but frankly speaking the audio it to bassy and that makes the music listening a little un-natural though you have options to long press one bud and change the sound but as you expect there minor improvements. trebles= Trebels or high tones are also done nothing great than excellent but still it sounds good and you also get a great depth effect. Vocals= There are chances of even further improvements in this one because i have noticed that if volume is not to high it feels shrilly unless you are a person who does like to listen any music with high volume. So to summarise i would say it is a good wirless earphones but not without some of its shades of flaws that i have mentioned above and i am able to justify this for that price of about 2,500 Rs.
